Christopher Reutz' Bio Page

Christopher Reutz was born in San Diego, California in 1972. He attended Crawford High School and San Diego State University, graduating in 1995 with a BA in Economics. While at SDSU, he started off as a computer science major and should have stuck with it.

In 2005, Christopher married his wife Jennifer. They adopted their son Eli in 2010. Christopher's family includes his dogs Jeff and Dory and they all happily live in North Park.
